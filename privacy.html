What We Collect

When you submit a trip inquiry or sign up for our newsletter, we collect information you provide voluntarily, including your name, email address, travel dates, and trip preferences. We do not collect payment information directly — all payments are processed through Stripe or Gumroad.

We use the information you provide to:

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Email Communications
