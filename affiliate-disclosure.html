The Carry-On Co. participates in affiliate programs, including the Travelpayouts network, which covers flights, hotels, tours, and car rentals. This means that when you click certain links on this site and make a purchase or booking, we may earn a small commission — at no additional cost to you.

Our Commitment to You

Affiliate relationships do not influence our recommendations. Every hotel, flight route, or service we recommend is based on genuine quality, value, and personal experience. We recommend the same options we would book for ourselves — the commission is a bonus, not the reason for the recommendation.

We never recommend a product, service, or destination solely because it pays a higher commission. If a non-affiliate option is genuinely better for your trip, we will tell you.
